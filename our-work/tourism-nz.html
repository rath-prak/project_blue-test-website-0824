Tourism New Zealand — Insights Tool

When an industry is in crisis, and market trends are uncertain – the only way forward is to have the latest information at your fingertips to make fast, informed decisions.

"Tourism was the first industry to be affected by the pandemic and will be the last to recover."

— Stuff.co.nz, December 2021

The Challenge

When the COVID-19 pandemic forced the closure of international borders, New Zealand’s lucrative international tourism industry was shut down virtually overnight.

Industry leader, Tourism New Zealand, were committed to supporting the Regional Tourism Organisations and Economic Development Agencies to better understand the domestic visitor market. In particular, they wanted to help these stakeholders understand the new domestic visitation number trends.

The Data

Data Ventures were able to use their expertise in data sourcing to bring together encrypted, anonymised and aggregated mobile phone data from two major telecommunication providers to get an understanding of population density on a daily basis at a regional level. From there, using some clever Data Modelling & Design, Data Ventures were able to segment the population by local residents, domestic visitors and any remaining international visitors.

The insights

With the data sorted, it was then about ensuring that Tourism New Zealand’s stakeholders could access and draw insights from the data easily. Data Ventures' product team specialises in creative, stylish and practical data visualisations, and were able to create an interactive Insights Tool. The Insights Tool provided a user-friendly interface for the tourism stakeholders to easily engage with the data through a near real-time dashboard.

This enabled them to make informed decisions on behalf of their local tourism operators around marketing and infrastructure. It also meant the Regional Tourism Organisations could disseminate the new visitation trends to assist individual tourism operators in understanding their new market conditions and the adaptations they may need to make to their business model.

Insights tool — displaying daily estimates at noon segmented by domestic and international visitors.

Insights tool — displaying monthly averages segmented by domestic and international visitors.

Ultimately, the result was a new dataset and an Insights Tool which provided a way for Tourism New Zealand to support their stakeholders during a very difficult time. This new dataset, sourced by Data Ventures, allowed their stakeholders the ability to understand the new trends of domestic visitation and to make informed business decisions.

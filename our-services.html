our services

As a multidisciplinary team, we have a wide range of essential skills that are key to the success of every data project. Here are just some of the services we offer.●

Data Sourcing

Stats NZ data

The right data can unlock efficiency, insights and new opportunities. Data Ventures’ great understanding of the data available at Stats NZ means we can find the best information publicly available so you can make the right decisions.

Public sector data

As part of the Data System Leadership group Data Ventures understands what data is available across the public sector to inform your organisational challenges. We are able to detail what datasets may be available, scope the feasibility and provide a roadmap for delivery.

Private sector data

With proven experience working with private sector data providers, we are able to assist in exploring what data might be available across the private sector. We know what is required to build trusted relationships, sound agreements and good operational processes which are all essential in sourcing private sector data.

Data Insights & Design

Analysis & Insights

Data Ventures’ has the skills to take large, disparate and messy data and create succinct and clear analysis. We are then able to curate the essential and useful insights that are relevant to the challenges, opportunities or questions that our customers face.

Methodological Design & Review

With world leading methodologists within Data Ventures and Stats NZ, we bring a global best practice and statistical rigour to methodological development. Whether it is a review or starting from scratch, we are excited to get stuck in.

Data Modelling & Design

Using the latest architectural approaches, Data Ventures is able to design effective and integrated data models, which produce useful insights. In an environment where there are many disparate datasets, Data Ventures is able to create order and value.

Data Visualisations

Data Ventures has a strong reputation for original and inventive data visualisations. We prioritise effective communication of data, led by our in-house designer, we ensure your data visualisations accentuate the key insights and engage the key audiences.

Data Infrastructure

Data Governance & Security

Keeping data safe should always be the top priority. Data Ventures has experience working with sensitive and difficult data, and applying the appropriate standards and policies to ensure it is kept safe, secure and privacy is maintained.

Data Processing & Analytics

With a team of skilled data scientists and analysts, Data Ventures creates “technology-agnostic” solutions to collect and translate data into usable information for organisations.

Cloud Development & Deployment

Cloud computing can have an exponential impact on an organisations approach to data management and use. Data Ventures has experience in cloud development and deployment, and specialises in guiding organisations on their cloud journey, especially in a public sector context.

Product Development

Insights Tool Development

Effective insights tools create a practical way for users, with a range of technical abilities, to interact, understand and make decisions from data. Utilising our multidisciplinary team, and purpose-built tech stack, Data Ventures is able to design, develop, deliver, host and support brilliant interactive insights tools.

Bespoke Dashboards

Whether dashboards are useful and used, depends on how targeted the information is to the organisational customers. Data Ventures are experts and engaging stakeholders, understanding the information required for decision making and creating dashboards that serve that purpose.

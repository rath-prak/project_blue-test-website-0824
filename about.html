Who we are

Data Ventures is a business unit of Stats NZ and operates as a full-service project-based data consultancy serving the government data system.

Learn more about who we are

We provide practical support to government agencies through high quality, innovative, best practice data solutions. Public good is our number one priority. Our work focuses on providing new and effective data solutions that can benefit multiple public agencies and the wider data system as a whole.

OUR STORY – Data Ventures & Stats NZ

Data Ventures began in early 2018 as a commercial business unit of Stats NZ under then CEO Liz MacPherson. Designed to be an incubator for innovative data products for the good of Aotearoa, the initial team was led by Drew Broadley.

Stats NZ building at 8 Willis Street, Wellington

Our fisrt product

A new look at NZ’s population. Population Density captures the number of people who work, live, and play in a given area every hour.

Population Density Product

Data Ventures worked together with major telecommunication companies in New Zealand. Each provider gave Data Ventures access to an aggregated and anonymised sum total of mobile devices down to suburb in New Zealand, across different time periods.

From there, we use Stats NZ expertise to develop population estimates that represent not just the number of mobile devices, but all of New Zealand's population — including visiting, residing and population that don't own a mobile phone.

our Process

Since the creation of our first product, Data Ventures has done a bunch of amazing consultancy work with customers across New Zealand. We find that the challenges faced across the data system are often tied together by similar themes.

Typically we adopt a straightforward approach to addressing long standing data challenges, and testing unique and useful solutions.

Our standard process

  • Identify and understand problem/challenge/opportunity
    Get the right people in the room, to deeply understand and agree on the challenge and its impacts.
  • Agree on scope and success criteria
    Get agreement across the key stakeholders on what a good result looks like, and what we can and can’t influence.
  • Derive potential solutions
    Utilise our expertise and knowledge of the data system to create unique, cutting edge options to solve the problem.
  • Recommended solution - proof of concept
    Get started! Get agreement on the right solution, and start putting it into action with a practical proof of concept.
  • Evaluating results
    Review the results! Does this meet the expected success criteria? Is the solution something that should be extended for greater impact? Are there any learnings to take forward and apply?
